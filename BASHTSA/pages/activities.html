Activities

Boyertown Senior High School TSA is constantly planning fun and exciting activities
for its members, both to raise money and have a good time.



Homecoming Carnival

The chapter ran two booths at the school homecoming carnival. One sold food and drinks, which students cooked and served. The other booth was a dart game which students also ran.



Spaghetti Dinner

The chapter held its third annual spaghetti dinner, in which students sell tickets to raise money for the state conference. Students managed the event which included drinks, all-you-can-eat spaghetti, salad, and dessert.



Bag Bingo Night

The chapter held a bingo night fundraiser with with vendors and designer bags as bingo prizes. Members made and sold food and assisted in running the bingo games.
