Awards

Boyertown Senior High School TSA has achieved at the Regional, State, and National level over the past
few years, securing countless awards for fundraising, community involvement, and competitive event success.

Regional Conference

At the Region 9 Conference, BASH-TSA had 63 teams or individuals place in the top 10 of their events.

Regional Inspiration Award

BASH-TSA won the Region 9 Inspiration Award - recognizing the Region 9 chapter most involved in their community.

State Conference

BASH-TSA had 36 teams place in the top 10 at the Pennsylvania State Conference, winning trophies in 3D Animation, Biotechnology Design, Children's Stories, Coding, Digital Video, Engineering Design, Essays on Technology, Fashion Design, Webmaster, Electronic Research and Experimentation, and Logo Design.

State Inspiration Award

BASH-TSA won the PA-TSA Inspiration Award - recognizing the PA-TSA chapter most involved in their community.

National Conference

BASH-TSA attended the National Conference in Orlando, Florida, and semifinalized in Webmaster, took 5th in Biotechnology Design, and 2nd in Software Development.

Purple Award

BASH-TSA received the National Service Project Purple Award for raising over $2,000 for the American Cancer Society.
