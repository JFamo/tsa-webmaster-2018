Uses

Plexor the Homebot will be able to manage chores around the house using sophisticated robotics, utilizing its vast array of sensors and motors. Using facial recognition and cognitive natural speech interpretation, Plexor can be a servant, assistant, or even a companion. Plexor will return to its own charging station when low on power, and requires only a WiFi connection. It is also able to connect to devices throughout your house using smart outlets, smart locks, and smart lights, allowing you to set a schedule for managing your home. Even without programming, Plexor's intelligence allows it to predict user commands and adjust home settings accordingly.
